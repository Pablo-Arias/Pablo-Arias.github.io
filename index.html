About

Hi! I'm Pablo Arias (Sarah). I am a French/Colombian Postdoctoral researcher working at both Lund University, in Sweden, and at IRCAM, in France. Lately, I've been interested in trying to hack human social interaction mechanisms using real time voice/face transformations. To do this, we've been developing a new experimental platform called DuckSoup, which will allow researchers to manipulate participants' voice and face in real time during social interactions.

I hold a a PhD in cognitive science from Sorbonne University, a Master of Engineering in digital technologies and multimedia from Polytech' Nantes and a Master Science in acoustics, signal processing and computer science applied to sound from IRCAM. You can find a complete list of my publictions here or follow me on twitter to keep up to date with my latest work.
